And the Rollercoaster Continues…

Oct 8th, 2013

Saturday was Installfest – I left the DaVinci Institute feeling exhilarated and excited about all of the “stuff” I added to my shiny new MacBook:

Homebrew – software that references beer? I’m in!

iTerm2 – feeling like a psuedo techie now

Google Chrome – adios Safari

AirParrot – hey, learning stuff I can use at home too!

Anki – who doesn’t love flashcards?

Evernote – this is definitely going to come in handy

Skitch – screen shots = money for a beginning student

Flycut – not proficient enough to dabble in this…yet

Moom – my favorite new app, can’t wait to learn the keyboard shortcuts

HipChat – misery loves company :)

Github – the sticker is so cool, this must be great

On Sunday I tackled the first few homework problems. These were – dare I say – easy? The exercises introduced concepts such as objects, variables, assignment. I was feeling confident and…smart?

At 10 o’clock on Monday night, I left the first class with heavy eyes and a heavy heart. Managed to keep up with the class (kinda-sorta) but wow, there is a lot to learn. We used Octopress to start our own blogs. I’ve always wanted to start a blog so I was eager to jump in.

Tonight I set out to finish what we started with the blog. After poking around for about 2 hours, I finally remembered how to do everything we did the night before using the terminal and RubyMine. Was I actually sleeping with my eyes open in class? :)
